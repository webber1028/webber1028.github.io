🌟 Key Features

1️⃣ 100% Pure Logic: Unique Solutions (No Guessing)

We despise trial and error. Every single puzzle is rigorously verified by algorithms to ensure a unique solution solvable entirely through logical deduction. Upon completion, the system automatically displays the specific solving techniques used for that level, helping you bridge the gap between amateur and master.

2️⃣ Tournament Grade Standards
  • Difficulty Calibration: Classic 9×9 puzzles are strictly capped at SE 4.0 (Sudoku Explainer) and below, perfectly aligning with international tournament standards.
  • Beginner Friendly: A dedicated 6×6 mode lets newcomers grasp core logic principles without the overwhelm of a full grid.
3️⃣ First-of-its-Kind: Bluetooth Local Multiplayer

Sudoku is no longer a solitary sport. Connect with friends via Bluetooth for real-time head-to-head battles:

  • Speed Race: A pure test of mental agility. Who finishes the grid first?
  • Cell Grab: A strategic battle for territory. Claim the grid, one cell at a time.
4️⃣ Diverse Variants & Global Challenges
  • Exotic Variants: Includes Diagonal, Killer, Odd/Even, and more. Our puzzle library is constantly expanding.
  • Global Rankings: Take on the Daily Puzzle and compete with players worldwide to claim your spot on the leaderboards.
